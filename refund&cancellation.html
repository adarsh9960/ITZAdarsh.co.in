Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Service-Based Model

We offer digital services like website development and SEO. Once work has started, partial or full refunds are not typically issued.

2. Project Cancellation

If you wish to cancel a project before work begins, you may request a refund. However, any transaction fees will be deducted.

3. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds will only be processed if:

  • Work has not started
  • Request is made within 24 hours of payment
  • No major resources have been allocated

4. Timeline

If approved, refunds will be processed within 5–7 working days through the original payment method.

5. Contact

Email us at contact@itzadarsh.co.in for any refund-related queries.
