About Us

Welcome to ITZAdarsh — where passion meets purpose.

I'm Adarsh, a self-taught web developer and gaming content creator from Bhayander, Mumbai. My journey began with a simple dream: to build powerful websites and entertain people with high-energy gaming content, all while balancing real-life challenges and responsibilities.

By day, I develop dynamic websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React JS — ensuring both functionality and a futuristic look. By night, I stream GTA V gameplay on YouTube, sharing laughs with friends and growing a gaming community, one short video at a time — no facecam, just pure fun.

As the sole earning member of my family, every project I take on is personal. Whether you're looking for a modern website, a digital presence, or even loan funding support, I'm here to help you turn your vision into reality.

Let’s build something amazing together — digitally or virtually!
